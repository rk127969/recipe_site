Chocolate Cake Recipe

"Here is is the fast and easy chocolate cake recipe. Enjoy!"


Difficulty Preperation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark Chocolate:200g

Sugar:200g

Butter:100g

Eggs:4

Flour:300g

Procedure

1

Melt chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix sugar eggs and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven at a perfect temperatures
